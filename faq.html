Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about Reward Club coins, earnings, cashouts, and security.

Reward Club is a premier cashback and loyalty program in India. We partner with over 1,500+ top retailers (like Amazon, Myntra, Swiggy, HPCL). When you shop at these brands through our app or website, they pay us an affiliate commission. We pass the majority of this commission directly to you as "Reward Club Coins" or cashback.
Reward Club Coins are the currency of our loyalty platform. Unlike other loyalty programs that complicate values, 4 Reward Club Coins = ₹1 in real currency. Once verified, you can withdraw your coins directly as Indian Rupees into your bank account.
Once you reach a minimum verified balance of ₹100, you can request a cashout. Go to the "Wallet" tab in the app, choose either bank account transfer (via NEFT/IMPS) or instant UPI transfer, enter your account details or UPI ID, and submit. Funds are processed in less than 24 hours.
