User Safety

Privacy Policy

Learn how we process your transactions, manage cookie IDs, and secure your personal data.

At Reward Club, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Reward Club and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at privacy@rewardclub.in.

1. Information We Collect

To run our cashback tracking systems, we need to collect specific transaction details, including:

  • Basic account details: Name, Email Address, and Phone Number.
  • Withdrawal details: UPI IDs or bank account numbers (processed via secure gateway partners).
  • Affiliate cookies: Session identifiers and clicking timestamp coordinates to link your order to your Reward Club account.

We do NOT collect or see your credit card details, passwords, CVVs, or personal financial details. When you buy, you enter payment credentials directly on the secure, certified payment gate of the merchant store (like Swiggy or Amazon).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected details to:

  • Track and verify your online purchases with partner brands.
  • Credit matching coins or cashback amounts to your wallet.
  • Deliver your cashouts directly to your designated bank accounts.
  • Send custom savings alert notifications and coupon deals.
  • Analyze usage metrics to optimize our website loading performance.

3. Cookie Tracking Systems

Like most cashback sites, Reward Club uses tracking cookies. When you click a brand's offer on our website, a secure click-through cookie is set on your browser. This cookie tells the retailer that you came from Reward Club so they pay us a commission, which we share with you. If you disable cookies on your browser, we will not be able to track your shopping and credit you cashback.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal details to third-party marketing companies. We only share transaction IDs and order totals with affiliate marketing network providers to verify sales. We may share bank details with authorized banking API integrations (like Razorpay or Cashfree) solely to execute your withdrawals.

5. Information Security

We use industry-standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption, firewalls, and regular vulnerability checks to keep your databases safe. Access to user directories is strictly limited to support representatives who need to review transaction errors.

6. Consent

By using our website or mobile application, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions.
