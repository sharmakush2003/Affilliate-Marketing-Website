1. Account Registration and Eligibility

To use our services, you must create a verified account. You agree to provide true, accurate, current, and complete information during registration. You must be at least 18 years of age to register and hold a bank account to cash out rewards in India.

2. Earnings, Tracking, and Cashbacks

Reward Club works as an intermediary program. We only credit cashback or points (Coins) once the transaction is fully confirmed and paid by our partner retailers. Furthermore, standard tracking times vary from 2 to 72 hours depending on the brand, and verification cycles can take up to 90 days (matching the refund windows of the retail store).

Standard coins valuation is set at 4 Coins = 1 Indian Rupee (INR). This conversion rate is fixed and will not fluctuate, but Reward Club reserves the right to alter loyalty rules, caps, or commission structures with a 15-day prior announcement on the app portal.
