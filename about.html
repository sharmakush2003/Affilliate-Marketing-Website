How We Make it Work

The Reward Club Business Model

We operate a transparent, consumer-first affiliate model where everyone wins. Here is how we fund your cashback rewards:

Whenever you use Reward Club to visit a retailer (like Amazon or Swiggy), we are acting as marketing partners. In return for bringing them a customer, the retailer pays us a pre-negotiated sales commission on your purchase total.

Instead of keeping this entire commission as corporate profit, we return up to 80% of it directly to you in the form of Reward Club Coins or Cashback. The remaining 20% covers our operational expenses, app maintenance, and database tracking. This creates a sustainable win-win-win model:

1. Retailers Get Sales

Top brands get customers, boost sales volumes, and pay Reward Club commissions for driving conversions.

2. Reward Club Tracks Earnings

Our automated tracking systems verify the purchase and allocate the commission points to your wallet.

3. You Get Direct Cash

Shoppers enjoy maximum discount values and withdraw actual cash back directly to their banks or UPI wallets.
